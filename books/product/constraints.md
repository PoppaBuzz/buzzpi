# Product Design Constraints

These constraints define the boundaries of BuzzPi as a product. They eliminate bad ideas by making the tradeoffs explicit before design begins.

- The user never sees an IP address. This is not a goal. It is a hard constraint.
- Pairing a new device takes under two minutes.
- The app works offline. Cloud is optional.
- Everything discoverable by tapping, not by reading a manual.
- Every error message is human-readable. Raw error codes never reach the user.
- No telemetry by default. No analytics. No tracking.
- First-time setup requires no terminal commands on the Pi.
- The app installs from Google Play or F-Droid without sideloading.
