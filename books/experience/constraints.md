# Experience Design Constraints

These constraints define how BuzzPi must feel. They are the non-negotiable qualities of every interaction.

- Every animation runs at 60 fps. No exceptions.
- Tap targets are at least 48dp. Every interactive element meets accessibility size guidelines.
- All text meets WCAG AA contrast ratio minimums.
- Every screen can be navigated without color perception.
- The app responds to touch within 16ms of the user's finger landing.
- Loading states are shown within 200ms. After 1 second, a progress indicator appears.
- Empty states are informative, not blank. Every empty screen explains why and what to do.
- Notifications are silent by default. Users opt into sound per category.
- Every microinteraction has a purpose. Decoration without function is removed.
- Copywriting is consistent across the entire platform. Terminology is defined in the Reference book and never varies.
